About Us

Shree Raja Rajeshwari Fast Food Center is a trusted name for quick, delicious meals in town. We specialize in freshly cooked rice varieties, Indo-Chinese favorites like Manchurian, and flavorful noodles — all prepared with quality ingredients and a focus on hygiene.
